💀 CONFESSION.EXE

You plug back in.

You whisper to the hacker:

“Post the real me.”

The files upload.
Old diary entries.
Private therapy session transcripts.
Everything you never told anyone—not even yourself.

The internet eats it alive.
Some say you’re brave.
Most say you’re broken.
A few say you made it all up for clout.

But the hacker?

They finally go silent.

You’re left with everything exposed.
And yet… weirdly free.

Your name becomes an urban legend:

"The One Who Confessed to the Cloud."

People start doing it too.
They call it “Going Full You.”

But your face still auto-fills in search bars…
Right next to the word “ruined.”

Paper Trail to Nowhere

You drive to the nearest FBI field office. The agent listens, stone-faced.

  • You hand them the flash drive.
  • They quarantine it. Ask questions. Take your devices.
  • Then they stop responding to your follow-ups.
"Your case is under internal review. Please do not contact us again."

Later that week, you get a bill from your ISP. Your bandwidth usage? 3 terabytes… yesterday.

Whatever you turned in—someone else turned on.

THE LESSON (Not Even the Authorities Can Save You From Everything.)
"Good intentions don’t always come with good outcomes. The chain of custody doesn’t mean chain of control."

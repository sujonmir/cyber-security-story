SET UP RECOVERY QUESTIONS

You set up recovery questions for all your accounts, ensuring you have a backup method to regain access in case you lose your phone or forget your passwords.

These questions can be a lifesaver, but you also make sure they're hard to guess.

"A good question is more secure than a weak answer."

What do you do next?

  1. Test your recovery questions to ensure they work.
  2. Update the recovery questions periodically to ensure their relevance.

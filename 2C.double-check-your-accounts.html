DOUBLE-CHECK YOUR ACCOUNTS

You log into your major accounts manually—banking, email, cloud storage— looking for anything unusual.

So far, nothing looks out of place… but a failed login attempt from a foreign IP shows up.

"Someone tried. They might try again."

What’s your move?

  1. Enable 2-Factor Authentication on every account.
  2. Change all your passwords immediately.
  3. Check if your info has leaked to the dark web.

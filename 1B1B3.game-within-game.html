The Game Within the Game

Your desktop changes.
All icons gone—except one.
“HackMe.html”

You open it. A pixelated game loads.
It stars you.
Every move you make—real or virtual—is mirrored by the character.
You try to close it. You can’t.

“To survive, finish the game. To fail, lose your name.”

CHOOSE YOUR FATE:

  1. Play the game carefully, following every rule.
  2. Try to break the game using your coding skills.
  3. Hold the power button. Force shutdown.

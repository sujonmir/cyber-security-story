Broken Flash Drive

You plug the broken flash drive into your laptop. It sputters and sparks before finally reading.

  • A folder appears, titled “Secrets.”
  • As you open it, dozens of encrypted files flash before your eyes, each labeled with a familiar name.
  • Your name is among them. Below, it says: “Delete them all, or we expose you.”

Your options:

  1. Delete all files and hope for the best.
  2. Report everything to the authorities.

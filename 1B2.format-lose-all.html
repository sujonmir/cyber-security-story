FORMAT EVERYTHING

In desperation, you decide to format everything. After all, it’s your only option left. The system starts wiping everything: documents, pictures, apps, memories.

You click through the prompts quickly, hoping for a miracle.

"WARNING: This action is irreversible. Are you sure you want to continue?"

You press YES.

Everything is gone. In the blink of an eye. Your files, your photos, your future... all wiped clean.

Then, another message appears:

"YOU CAN NEVER ESCAPE."

The hacker’s message. It’s too late. The trap was set before you even clicked.

Your options:

  1. Call IT Support—again.
  2. Report the attack to the authorities.
  3. Move on, knowing there's no going back.

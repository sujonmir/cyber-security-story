THE LAST ESCAPE

In a panic, you shut down your computer. The screen goes black, and for a moment, you think you’ve escaped.

But then, your phone buzzes. You unlock it.

"Your system has been compromised."

The message is from an unknown number. It’s followed by a link to your files— still encrypted, still locked.

You feel the walls closing in. The hacker isn’t just in your computer—they’ve infiltrated your phone, your life. Everything is connected, and there’s no easy way out.

You can’t escape the digital trap. It’s too late.

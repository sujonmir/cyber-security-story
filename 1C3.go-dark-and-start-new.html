GO DARK AND START NEW

You smash your phone. Burn your hard drives. Withdraw all your money in cash. It’s time to disappear.

But as you prepare to vanish, you realize this isn’t just about running. It’s about *why* they targeted you in the first place.

"You know something they don’t want getting out."

What do you do?

  1. Contact a journalist with your story before you vanish.
  2. Create a decoy identity to lure them away.
  3. Use every tool to completely erase your digital footprint.

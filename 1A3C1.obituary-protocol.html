🪦 OBITUARY PROTOCOL

You vanish.

A fake news article surfaces:

“Local cybersecurity consultant found dead in alleged home fire.”
“No foul play suspected.”

Your burner laptop melts in a barrel.
Dental records faked.
You bribe a contact to push the paperwork through.

Days later, your inbox pings—on a private server in Switzerland.

“Smart move. They’ll stop chasing ghosts.”

You live quietly.
Different name.
Off-grid.

No socials.
No spotlight.
No more threats.

But some nights,
you wake to the sound of your old ringtone.

It plays from nowhere.

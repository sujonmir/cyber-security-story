The Package Arrives

A knock.
You freeze.
There’s a box on your doorstep. No label. No return address.

Inside:

  • A VHS tape
  • A broken flash drive
  • A photo of you, asleep, taken from outside your window

Written in sharpie:

“One of these holds your freedom. The others, your undoing.”

Which will you try?

  1. Play the VHS tape
  2. Plug in the broken flash drive
  3. Scan the photo

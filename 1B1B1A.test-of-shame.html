The Test of Shame

“Question One.”

Your camera zooms in on your face.
You didn’t touch anything.

“What is the one thing you’ve done… that you’ve never told anyone?”

The screen displays a blinking cursor.
Waiting.
Judging..

You type something. Something real.
You hit Enter.
The voice returns—happier now.

“Good. Now… let’s go deeper.”

Your screen shows dozens of faces.
Family. Friends. Coworkers.
The voice asks:

“Pick one. Only one. The rest… we’ll break.”
  1. Choose someone you love. Save them.
  2. Refuse to choose. Try to crash the program.
